Our Story

LocoServe was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make it easier for people to find reliable local service providers in their community.

We noticed that finding trustworthy professionals for home services was often a challenge. People relied on word-of-mouth recommendations or took chances with unknown providers, often leading to disappointing experiences.

That's why we created LocoServe - a platform that connects skilled local service providers with customers who need their services, all while ensuring quality, reliability, and transparency.

Today, LocoServe has grown to serve thousands of customers across multiple cities, helping them find the right professionals for their needs while supporting local businesses and service providers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about LocoServe

How does LocoServe work?

LocoServe connects customers with local service providers. Customers can search for services, view provider profiles and reviews, and book services directly through our platform. Service providers can create profiles, list their services, and manage bookings.

How are service providers verified?

All service providers on LocoServe go through a verification process that includes identity verification, professional credentials check, and background screening. We also collect and display customer reviews to help maintain quality standards.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with a service?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not satisfied with a service, you can contact our customer support team, and we'll work with you and the service provider to resolve the issue. We also have a dispute resolution process for more complex situations.

How do I become a service provider on LocoServe?

To become a service provider, you need to create an account, complete your profile with your professional information, and go through our verification process. Once approved, you can list your services and start receiving booking requests.

Is there a fee to use LocoServe?

LocoServe is free for customers to use. Service providers pay a small commission on completed bookings. There are no upfront fees or monthly subscriptions for providers to join the platform.
