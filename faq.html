What is LocoServe?

LocoServe is a platform that connects customers with local service providers. We make it easy to find, book, and pay for services like plumbing, electrical work, cleaning, and more in your local area.

How does LocoServe work?

LocoServe connects customers with local service providers. Customers can search for services, view provider profiles and reviews, and book services directly through our platform. Service providers can create profiles, list their services, and manage bookings.

Where is LocoServe available?

LocoServe is currently available in over 50 cities across the United States. We're constantly expanding to new locations. You can check if we're available in your area by entering your zip code on our homepage.

Is there a fee to use LocoServe?

LocoServe is free for customers to use. Service providers pay a small commission on completed bookings. There are no upfront fees or monthly subscriptions for providers to join the platform.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team by emailing support@locoserve.com, calling (555) 123-4567 during business hours (Mon-Fri, 9am-6pm PST), or by using the contact form on our Contact page.

How do I book a service?

To book a service, search for the type of service you need, browse through available providers, select one that meets your requirements, choose a date and time, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

How are service providers verified?

All service providers on LocoServe go through a verification process that includes identity verification, professional credentials check, and background screening. We also collect and display customer reviews to help maintain quality standards.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes, you can cancel a booking through your account dashboard. Our cancellation policy varies depending on how close to the scheduled service time you cancel. Cancellations made more than 24 hours in advance typically receive a full refund.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with a service?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not satisfied with a service, you can contact our customer support team, and we'll work with you and the service provider to resolve the issue. We also have a dispute resolution process for more complex situations.

How do I leave a review?

After a service is completed, you'll receive an email prompting you to leave a review. You can also leave a review by going to your account dashboard, finding the completed service, and clicking on "Leave a Review."

How do I become a service provider on LocoServe?

To become a service provider, you need to create an account, complete your profile with your professional information, and go through our verification process. Once approved, you can list your services and start receiving booking requests.

What fees does LocoServe charge?

LocoServe charges a 10% commission on completed bookings. There are no upfront fees, monthly subscriptions, or hidden charges. You only pay when you earn.

How do I get more bookings?

To increase your bookings, make sure your profile is complete with detailed information about your services, add high-quality photos of your work, encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews, respond promptly to booking requests, and maintain a high quality of service.

When and how do I get paid?

Payments are processed within 48 hours after a service is completed and the customer confirms satisfaction. The funds are transferred directly to your linked bank account or payment method. You can track all your earnings in your provider dashboard.

Can I set my own schedule and prices?

Yes, as a service provider on LocoServe, you have full control over your schedule and pricing. You can set your availability, define your service area, and determine your rates for different services.

What payment methods are accepted?

LocoServe accepts all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), as well as PayPal and Apple Pay. Some service providers may also accept cash payments for certain services.

When am I charged for a service?

For most services, your payment method is authorized when you book the service, but you're only charged after the service is completed. For some larger jobs, a deposit may be required at the time of booking.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, there are no hidden fees on LocoServe. The price you see when booking a service is the price you'll pay, unless you and the service provider agree to additional work or services during the appointment.

How do refunds work?

If you cancel a service more than 24 hours in advance, you'll typically receive a full refund. For cancellations within 24 hours, a cancellation fee may apply. If a service provider cancels, you'll always receive a full refund. If you're unsatisfied with a completed service, contact our customer support team to discuss refund options.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, LocoServe uses industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your payment information. We never store your full credit card details on our servers. All payment processing is handled by trusted third-party payment processors that comply with PCI DSS standards.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the website, enter your email address and create a password, then follow the prompts to complete your profile information.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on "Login," then "Forgot Password." Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact customer support.

Can I have both a customer and provider account?

Yes, you can have both types of accounts with the same email address. When you log in, you'll be able to switch between your customer and provider profiles from your account dashboard.

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile information, log in to your account, go to your account dashboard, and click on "Edit Profile." From there, you can update your personal information, contact details, and preferences.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, log in, go to your account settings, and select "Delete Account." Please note that account deletion is permanent and will remove all your data from our system. If you have any active bookings or outstanding payments, you'll need to resolve those before deleting your account.
